When discussing cool functions, don't forget to consider the walk-dog (start, stop) function.

  • It is great for people who own a dog.
  • If you would like to acquire a dog, you can set the stop point of another person's dog to your own house.
  • If you have an unwanted dog, you can set the stop point somewhere other than your own house.
